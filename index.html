About me.

Hi, I'm, Jamie, this is my personal blog and portfolio. I am a multi-skilled web developer with a passion for innovative solutions and clean user experiences.

I'm inspired by clean, minimalistic design. Whether it be digital or physical, I believe design is all about solving problems in the simplest possible manner.

I have over 12 years of experience from North Wales. I have a passion for pixel perfection, clean code, and semantics. I currently work for MoneySuperMarket as a web developer.
