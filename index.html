About me.

Hi, I'm, Jamie, this is my personal blog and portfolio. I am a multi-skilled web developer from North Wales, with a passion for innovative solutions and clean user experiences. I currently work for MoneySuperMarket.com as a web developer.

I'm inspired by clean, minimalistic design. Whether it be digital or physical, I believe design is all about solving problems in the simplest possible manner. I am fully proficient in HTML, CSS & Javascript and not afraid to learn something new like SASS, Java or AngularJS. I have over 12 years of experience. I have a passion for pixel perfection, clean code, and semantics.

When not making pixelated things I like to take pretty pictures, such as the cloudy sunset in my hero image and a bit of hiking. I recently travelled to Tanazania to climb a small hill called Kilimanjaro (you may have hread of it) and recently to Morrocco's Atlas Mountains.
